La Sacramental is a new independent vinyl label based in Barcelona, focused on gothic rock, darkwave, and deathrock from the 1980s and 1990s.

We exist to bring back records that have been out of reach for too long — in small, carefully produced pressings, with the attention these bands deserve.

Something is coming. We're not ready to say what yet, but we think it's worth the wait.
